Applications of Tree Data structure:

1. To represent hierarchial Data
    - Organization structure
    - Folder structure
    - xml/html content (JSON Objects)
    - In OOP (Inheritance)
2. Binary search trees
3. Heaps
4. B and B+ trees in DBMS
5. spanning and short spanning trees in computer networks
6. parse trees and expression trees in compilers
7. Tries
8. Suffix trees
9. Binary indexed tree
10. Segment tree

Tree Traversal:
1. BFS (Bredth first traversal)
2. DFS (Depth first traversal)
    - Inorder (left->root->right)
    - Preorder (root->left->right)
    - Postorder (left->right->root)
    
ex:             10
              /    \
            20      30

    Inorder: 20 10 30
    Preorder: 10 20 30
    Postorder: 20 30 10
